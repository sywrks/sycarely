About SyCarely

Established 2025, SyCarely delivers affordable, patient-centred home healthcare across Klang and Shah Alam. Our licensed clinicians bring hospital-grade expertise to the comfort of your home—so recovery fits your life, not the other way around.

Our Services

  • Home Nursing Visits – wound care, medication management, vital-sign monitoring
  • Physiotherapy & Rehab – post-surgical, stroke, sports and geriatric programmes
  • Doctor Tele-consultation – video calls plus prescription delivery within 3 hrs
  • Elderly Wellness Checks – fall-risk assessment, chronic-disease coaching
  • Medical Equipment Rental – hospital beds, oxygen concentrators, mobility aids

Coverage: Klang • Shah Alam (Selangor)
